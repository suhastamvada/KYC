The 2024 NEET crisis showed that India debates education reform in courts,
on streets, and in hashtags — everywhere except a shared, structured place
where ideas are proposed, tested against evidence, voted on, and refined
into something a policymaker could pick up and use.

KYC — Know Your Citizen — is that place: a living document of reforms,
drafted in public, with every vote and every change auditable.
