# KYC · The Draft — Preamble

**Version 0.1 · Unamended · Last updated: (set on launch)**

This is a living document of proposed reforms to India's education system,
written and revised in public. It consolidates proposals that have earned
sustained support and survived factual scrutiny through the process described
in [GOVERNANCE.md](../GOVERNANCE.md).

It is a citizen initiative. Nothing here is official policy, and the document
claims no authority beyond the quality of its evidence and the visibility of
its consensus. Its ambition is to be useful: a well-sourced, current,
version-controlled reference that any policymaker, journalist, court, or
committee can consult and cite.

## Structure

Each chapter covers one reform area:

- **01 — Examination integrity** (NEET and national entrance examinations)
- Further chapters (school education, higher-education regulation, the
  coaching industry, teacher capacity) will be added as proposals in those
  areas mature.

## Status of content

Sections are marked with one of:

- **[Merged]** — earned inclusion through the governance process
- **[Seed]** — placed by maintainers at launch to demonstrate format and
  give the community something to react to; seeds carry no special status
  and can be amended or removed by the same process as anything else
