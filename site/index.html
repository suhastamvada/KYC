How it works

Anyone can submit a proposal using a required structure: problem, evidence, proposed reform, tradeoffs, and affected parties. The community votes and argues in public. Once a month, proposals with sustained support and no unaddressed factual objections are merged into the Draft under published governance rules, with every decision logged in a changelog. Vote counts inform merges; they never automate them.
